In 2002, the MoST Endowment was established at 09 different universities of Pakistan including NED University, by the Ministry of Science & Technology and Telecommunication. The overall objective of the Endowment project is to consolidate and expand the Research and Development Programmes in the areas relating to I.T. & Telecommunication Sector.

•To strengthen Postgraduate Research Programmes leading to Masters and Doctorate Degrees in various areas relating to I.T. & Telecommunication Sector.
•To produce three (03) Ph.D. and Twelve (12) M.Engg/MCIT in disciplines relating to I.T. & Telecommunication Sector per year.
•To undertake Industrial Research Projects which would be substantially strengthen University-Industry liaison.


Prof. Agathe Merceron (Feb 2010- September 2010), Professor of Computer Science, Beuth University, Berlin Germany was appointed as Professor of Computer Science.

Dr. Shamsul Haque (July 2011-July 2013) was appointed as Professor of Computer Science.

Dr. Najeed A Khan from Computer Science and Software Engineering department is looking after the MoST Endowment affairs as “Executive Officer”.

Our Mission

Our mission is to combine knowledge, hard work and enthusiasm in the areas of computer science & software engineering to be able to contribute to the software products development and research activities in our country.

Our Objective

The objective of this centre is to carry out research and development in the areas of computer science & software engineering. The CSRD will supervise PhD and MS theses and undergraduate projects in these areas.
